# McRAYGOR Premium Website Re-design: Figma Prompt for Senior UI/UX Designer

**Context:**
McRAYGOR is a leading manufacturer of municipal and industrial waste handling equipment, with over 25 years of legacy. We are re-designing our digital presence to serve two main audiences via two distinct experiences: an **Indian Website (IW)** and a **Global Website (GW)**. 
We need a world-class, premium industrial aesthetic that competes with top-tier foreign B2B brands (e.g., Caterpillar, John Deere, Kärcher industrial), moving away from typical template-driven Indian B2B sites.

**Role & Objective:**
As a Senior UI/UX Designer, your goal is to design a high-end, conversion-optimized, and visually striking interface for the McRAYGOR websites. The design must convey engineering capability, manufacturing scale, and uncompromising reliability. 

## 1. Core UX/UI Philosophy & Aesthetic
- **Visual Style:** Premium industrial, clean, modern, and minimal. Avoid cluttered layouts or "cheap" template looks. 
- **Typography:** Strong visual hierarchy. Use sans-serif, modern, corporate fonts that feel confident and engineered (e.g., Inter, Roboto, or similar modern geometric/neo-grotesque typefaces).
- **Motion & Micro-interactions:** 
  - *Indian Site:* Sober, purpose-driven motion. Avoid overly flashy effects. Performance-first.
  - *Global Site:* Richer, premium visual language with controlled, slow, confident animations. Industrial aesthetic.
- **Imagery:** High-resolution photography of machines, real projects, and manufacturing. Utilize Indian user behavior preferences by prioritizing visuals and videos over long blocks of text. Text should be highly scannable.

## 2. Navigation & Header Design
- **Pre-Header:** Must feature a region selector (Asia Pacific, Europe, etc.) and a clear "Visit Our Global Website" CTA (or vice-versa depending on the site).
- **Main Header:**
  - **Logo:** Clean placement. Need space next to it for a "Celebrating 25 Years" badge.
  - **Menu Links:** Home, About Us, Products, Spares, Government & Tender Support, Contact Us.
  - **Products Mega Menu:** Large, square-style mega dropdown. Categorized clearly into: Liquid Waste, Solid Waste, Industrial Vacuum Cleaning, and Special Purpose Equipment.
  - **Contact & CTAs:** Phone icon (+91 8447745599), Email icon. Strong CTA button: "Download 2026 Company Profile".

## 3. Key Pages & Layout Sections to Design

### A. Homepage (High Priority)
- **Hero Section:** Full-width high-res image slider showing machines. Needs a confident, text-based overlay introducing the brand as a "Manufacturer of Municipal and Industrial Waste Handling Equipment".
- **Quantifying Excellence (Stats Section):** Smoothly animated, dynamic metric counters (Years in Business: 25+, Total Machines Manufactured, Machines Running Worldwide, Countries Served, Projects Completed).
- **Clientele Showcase:** Slow-floating, two-column marquee slider displaying esteemed client logos (Private, Govt, and International).
- **Manufacturing Expertise:** Animated block detailing scale, quality control, and testing.

### B. Product Detail Pages (PDP)
- **Crucial Elements:** Large product imagery/video gallery. 
- **Structure:** Clear separated sections for Product Overview, Key Specifications, Applications.
- **CTAs:** "Download Brochure" (PDF) and a simple, frictionless "Request a Quote" sticky or inline form.

### C. About Us / Legacy / Vision
- Convey the 25+ year legacy creatively. Emphasize the 2019 re-establishment (if relevant to the narrative), engineering depth, and the vision of cleaner cities and mechanized sanitation.

### D. R&D and CSR Initiatives
- **R&D Page:** Needs to feel real and in-house. Sections for testing, validation, and patented products.
- **CSR Page:** Tone must be respectful, responsible, and impact-driven. Focus on eliminating manual scavenging and supporting sanitation workers' dignity.

### E. Global Presence & Contact
- **Map Visualization:** A world map (static or lightly animated) showing the global export footprint.
- **Contact Forms:** Minimalist UI. Include floating WhatsApp and Phone buttons across the site. Form fields restricted to Name, Phone, and Brief Requirement (no CAPTCHA visible).
- **Footer:** Clean layout with quick links, contact info, Google map embed for plant location, and the mandatory legal copyright snippet.

## 4. Deliverables Expected
1. **Wireframes:** Mobile-first structural wireframes for the Homepage, a Category Page, and a Product Detail Page.
2. **UI High-Fidelity Mockups (Desktop & Mobile):**
   - Homepage (Indian vs Global variant differences)
   - Mega Menu Expanded State
   - Product Detail Page
   - Contact & Support Pages
3. **Design System:** Colors, Typography, Component Library (Buttons, Inputs, Cards), and specific animation/transition documentation.

*Please ensure all designs focus on Core Web Vitals (fast perceived loading) and a seamless Mobile-first experience with zero layout breakage.*
